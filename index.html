About CJB

Hi, I'm Calum

I am the sole trader of CJB Carpentry.

My career began back when I was still at school helping out at weekends and during holidays when I could. I have always had a huge passion for carpentry and anything wood related. I love to create and build for a customers and fulfil their requirements to the highest standard. I am fully qualified to an advanced level with City & Guilds certifications. I have completed a full apprenticeship and since the age of 16 I have been in full time employment. I have always worked in the carpentry sector with experience in kitchens, pubs, hotels, banks, hospitals, private & commercial accommodations and even universities.

During the Covid restrictions of 2021, I decided to part ways with the employment lifestyle and set up on my own. I am a fully insured self trader now and always keen for the next project, whether you have a clear idea in mind or need help to create something to suit your needs, I will be happy to help. I avoid websites like Checkatrade and MyBuilder etc, due to my standard of work always bringing in new clients through word of mouth. If you are looking for a honest, hardworking and trustworthy carpenter, you've come to the right place.
